Stories of Sustainable Development

Something about how these stories tell how AKF does SDG.

In 2015, 193 nations agreed on the Sustainable Development Goals, which provide a global vision for a better world. The 17 goals are bold and ambitious. Ending extreme poverty by 2030 is at their core, for a healthy planet and a peaceful world. It will take a collective effort and partnerships with governments to achieve this and create a better future. Read more about the Sustainable Development Goals here.
